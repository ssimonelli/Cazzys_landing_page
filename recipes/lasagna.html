Lasagna is pretty tasty, and is one of my favorite dishes. I'm sure if you try this recipe, it'll be one of your favorite dishes too!!

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bacon for some reason
  • Cheese (get creative!)
  • Lack of Taste Buds

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Boil some water for the pasta, and cook the noodles until just about "el dental"
  2. Get that tomato sauce nice and warm
  3. call your mom, she hasn't heard from you in a while
  4. get that cheese out, and crumble it into workable pieces
  5. layer the sauce, then pasta, then cheese in strips inside of an oven safe casserole dish
  6. place into the oven at 100000 degrees C
  7. when it's done, take it out and cut into small pieces. eat immediately.
